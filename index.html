Safe

Despite the articles and news you might have seen, wind farms do not have a major impact on birds and bats. The chances of it happening are low, and the overall numbers killed are low. Climate change, power lines, and stray cats pose a bigger risk to birds than wind turbines do.

Job Creation. Invest in the Future.

The creation of turbines and the facilities required to run, monitor, and maintain them bring a wide variety of job opportunities to the surrounding areas. Technicians, architects, and contractors are just some of the jobs needed to make wind energy happen.

Global Industry. Worldwide Adoption.

Wind energy is a global phenomenon. In the United States, only 4% percent of our power is generated by wind energy. In some european nations, wind farms generate almost half of their total power. Wind power has recently seen adoption in India, and other countries in Asia.
